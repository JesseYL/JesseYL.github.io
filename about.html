About Me

Welcome! I am Yulong Jiao 焦雨龙, a passionate cartographer and geographer from the beautiful coastal city of Qingdao 青岛, China 中国. I'm currently pursuing BS degrees in Cartography & GIS and People-Environment Geography at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My academic journey combines my love for spatial analysis, storytelling, and understanding the dynamic relationship between people and their environment.

Beyond academics, I am someone who embraces life's richness. Whether it's traveling to new places, savoring different cuisines, or cheering for my favorite soccer team FC Barcelona, I find joy in exploring the world and its stories. Racing is another thrill that fuels my passion, adding an adrenaline rush to my adventures.

What truly excites me is the art of visual storytelling. Through my work, I aim to turn data into compelling narratives that resonate with audiences and spark curiosity. I believe maps and visuals have the power to connect people, uncover insights, and inspire action.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio! Feel free to explore my projects, and let's connect to share ideas and stories.
